Build / update the Staff Dashboard for a hotel task management system.
This dashboard is used by staff on mobile and desktop web.
Do NOT change authentication or business logic outside staff scope.

Goal:
Create a fast, distraction-free interface where staff can view and complete their assigned tasks with minimal interaction.

ROLE RULES (STRICT)

- Staff sees ONLY their own department

  - Reassign tasks
  - View other staff
  - Access stats or admin pages
  - Change task priority
  - See business settings
